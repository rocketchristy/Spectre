The NextGen Trading Card Game (NXTCG) features exclusive collectible cards inspired by the Rocket Software NextGen Academy and its Rocketeers. The platform empowers users to buy and sell new cards and related products, creating a dynamic marketplace for fans and collectors.

ECOMAPP is a Db2 13 for z/OS database for NXTCG. It is compromised of 15 normalized tables with full referential integrity and indexed access paths. Administration is driven by source-controlled DDL and JCL pipelines for build, initialization, statistics, grants, and teardown.

Key Features:

  • Complete SKU-based product catalog (4,824 trading card variants)
  • Multiplier-based dynamic pricing system
  • 16 normalized tables with full referential integrity
  • Performance indexes on all critical access paths
  • Split initialization files for mainframe line length compliance
  • Automated JCL for all database operations

Product Catalog:

  • 80 Individual Cards × 60 modifier combinations = 4,800 variants
  • 24 Mystery Products × 1 variant each = 24 variants
  • Total: 4,824 sellable SKUs
Note: NXTCG is planning to extend their future card catalog to NextGen Academies world-wide.

Database Components:

  • Database: ECOMDB01
  • Tablespaces: 15 tablespaces, one per table for flexibility.
  • Tables: USERS, TOKENS, SERIES, STYLES, STYLE_MODIFIERS, PRODUCT_TYPES, PRODUCT_VARIANTS, VARIANT_IMAGES, INVENTORY, CARTS, CART_ITEMS, ADDRESSES, ORDERS, ORDER_ADDRESSES, ORDER_ITEMS
  • Indexes: Primary key indexes + business key indexes

Db2 Common Codes

RC=0: Complete success - all DDL executed successfully

RC=4: Warning - generally safe to continue

RC=8: SQL error - review SYSTSPRT

RC=12+: Severe error - STOP and investigate
